Subject: Feature Request: Real-time Driver Tracking & Notifications for Logistics Platform

Dear Development Team,

We need to add a new driver tracking and notification system to our current logistics application. 

Currently, our customers have to manually refresh the page to see where their delivery driver is, or call support. We want to update this to show the driver's location on a map in real-time, updating every 10 seconds.

Additionally, we need automatic notifications:
1. When the driver starts the trip (SMS and Email).
2. When the driver is within 2 kilometers of the delivery address (Push notification to the customer's mobile app, saying "Your delivery is arriving soon!").
3. When the delivery is completed or if there's a delay.

The tracking data needs to be highly secure. Drivers should only be tracked when they are active on a shift. We also need to make sure this doesn't drain the driver's phone battery, as our drivers use standard Android devices.

We want to roll this out next month. Let us know if you need any clarification.

Best regards,

Operations Director, FastRoute Logistics
